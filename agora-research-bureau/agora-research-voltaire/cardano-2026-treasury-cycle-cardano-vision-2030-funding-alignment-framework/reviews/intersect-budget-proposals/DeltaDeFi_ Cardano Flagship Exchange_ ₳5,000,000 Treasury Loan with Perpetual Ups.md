## AI Rationale

The proposal’s core objective is to build and harden critical Cardano infrastructure: specifically a Hydra-based, low-latency order-book exchange DEX. This aligns directly with **Pillar 1: Infrastructure & Research Excellence**, which emphasizes keeping Cardano “secure, fast, and interoperable” and shipping production-grade infrastructure. The proposal explicitly funds “hardening and scale-up” of the Hydra-based exchange and plans open-source artifacts and decentralization milestones, which are infrastructure work.

By contrast, other pillars are less central. The loan’s revenue-share structure does create a lasting income stream for ADA holders, addressing long-term treasury health, but this financial mechanism is a secondary effect rather than the proposal’s primary goal. It is not primarily focused on user or developer adoption campaigns, governance processes, or community engagement activities.

Therefore, Infrastructure & Research is the best single pillar to justify the proposal, with Sustainability & Resilience as a secondary benefit from its repayable-loan structure.

## Classification Notes

### Primary Classification Justification

The proposal’s deliverables center on **technical infrastructure development**. It commits to making the DeltaDeFi exchange production-grade on Hydra L2, a direct contribution to Cardano’s infrastructure layer. The text explicitly highlights that the proposal “funds hardening and scale-up” of this Hydra-based exchange, and promises open-source smart contracts, multi-language clients, and decentralization milestones. These are concrete infrastructure outputs related to L2 integration, performance, security, and production readiness that serve Pillar 1’s mission of keeping Cardano secure, fast, and interoperable.

None of the core objectives involve community programs, governance innovation, or adoption marketing. Thus, Infrastructure & Research is the natural primary pillar.

### Manual Review Reason

Not required.

### Additional Notes

The proposal’s financial structure, namely a loan with perpetual ADA revenue share, does align with Pillar 5 by strengthening treasury sustainability, but the main intent remains the development and hardening of the exchange infrastructure. The proposer also argues primary alignment with Pillar 1. No other pillar, including Governance, Community, or Adoption, is strongly supported enough by the proposal text to displace Pillar 1 as the Primary Pillar.
