## AI Rationale

The proposal’s primary strategic purpose is to increase Cardano DeFi usage by integrating Cardano liquidity into the Houdiniswap aggregator through same-chain swaps, cross-chain swaps, bridge routes, wallet support, and API-based routing. This maps most directly to **Pillar 2: Adoption & Utility**, especially the framework’s focus on DeFi, payments, improved user experience, and practical non-speculative utility. 

Although the proposal is technical in implementation, its core objective is not protocol hardening, client diversity, L1 scalability, or research infrastructure. The technical work serves an application-layer adoption goal: making Cardano assets easier to swap, route, bridge, and integrate into wallets and third-party products. ## Supporting Evidence

### Evidence 1

The proposal states that it will integrate “Cardano Defi liquidity into Houdiniswap” and enable “both same-chain and cross-chain swap functionality.”

### Evidence 2

The proposal identifies three integration pathways: NEAR Intents for cross-chain ADA swaps, Minswap for native same-chain Cardano swaps, and Wanchain for cross-chain transfers into the Cardano ecosystem.

### Evidence 3

The Minswap work package is intended to let “ADA holders and Cardano-native token holders” swap directly within Cardano, increasing “on-chain Cardano DEX volume.”

### Evidence 4

The proposal states that Houdiniswap can contribute to Cardano volume and user activity KPIs by driving monthly swap volume, onboarding active users executing ADA swaps, tracking conversion rates, and reporting monthly metrics such as USD volume, unique users, and success rates.

### Evidence 5

The proposal plans to provide its API to wallets and other Cardano products so they can access same-chain, cross-chain, and private swap functionality.

## Classification Notes

### Primary Classification Justification

**Adoption & Utility** was selected because the proposal’s main justification is increased Cardano DeFi activity, improved liquidity access, lower swap friction, cross-chain asset movement, wallet compatibility, and measurable user/volume growth.

**Infrastructure & Research** was not selected as the Primary Pillar because the proposal does not primarily improve Cardano’s base protocol, node/client diversity, L1/L2 infrastructure, post-quantum readiness, threat recovery, or core research capacity. The integrations are technical, but they are product-level DeFi and aggregator integrations.

**Community & Ecosystem Growth** was not selected as the Primary Pillar because references to external users, builders, partners, and ecosystem visibility are secondary consequences of the swap-routing product. The proposal does not primarily focus on education, talent acquisition, localized engagement, public narrative, or community development.

### Manual Review Reason

Not applicable.

### Additional Notes

No Secondary Pillar was assigned because the apparent secondary effects, including ecosystem exposure, developer composability, and interoperability narrative, are not substantial enough to outweigh or stand independently from the proposal’s core Adoption & Utility purpose.
