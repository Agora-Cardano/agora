## AI Rationale

The **Amplify Cardano** proposal centers on bolstering the Cardano ecosystem by directly supporting developer-led projects and community initiatives. Its core objectives are to *accelerate marquee ecosystem projects* by helping 3–5 top projects reach leadership positions and Tier-1 exchange listings, and to *nurture grassroots contributors* by supporting 100+ smaller projects through an incubation pipeline.

In addition, it establishes a large fund for *community-led marketing and events*. These activities all focus on expanding global engagement, showcasing Cardano’s value, and growing the developer community, which aligns squarely with the **Community & Ecosystem Growth** pillar.

The proposal is not primarily about infrastructure improvements, on-chain use-case adoption, or governance process changes. Instead, it emphasizes community collaboration, marketing, and ecosystem development. This direct emphasis on engaging builders and users through events and funding new projects makes Community & Ecosystem Growth the best fit for the primary pillar.

## Classification Notes

### Primary Classification Justification

The proposal’s goals are fundamentally about building and showcasing the Cardano community and developer ecosystem. It funds an **Ecosystem Accelerator** and a **Community-Led Marketing & Events Fund**, which are explicitly community and ecosystem activities.

These align with Pillar 4, Community & Ecosystem Growth, from the Cardano 2030 framework, which emphasizes driving global engagement, cultivating a developer base, and demonstrating ecosystem value.

The proposal’s focus is *not* on Pillar 1, Infrastructure, or Pillar 3, Governance. Although it mentions Tier-1 listings and user engagement, it does not directly target Pillar 2, Adoption & Utility, verticals or UX improvements. Instead, it supports projects and marketing that indirectly boost adoption.

Therefore, Community & Ecosystem Growth is the dominant pillar that best describes this program’s strategic purpose.
