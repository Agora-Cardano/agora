## AI Rationale

Nula’s primary purpose is to introduce a usable DeFi/payment primitive on Cardano: token streaming for vesting, payroll, DAO grants, escrow, structured payouts, and recurring value transfer. That maps most directly to **Pillar 2: Adoption & Utility**, which focuses on non-speculative utility, high-value verticals such as DeFi and payments, improved user experience, enterprise-grade security, and developer experience. The proposal’s major deliverables, including the Plutus validator, web app, ticker bot, SDK, and adoption fee subsidy, all support making this financial primitive usable by individuals, DAOs, enterprises, wallets, custodians, and other dApps.  

Although the proposal repeatedly describes Nula as “infrastructure,” it is not primarily aligned with **Infrastructure & Research** as defined by Vision 2030, because it does not target L1 protocol improvements, L2 integration, cross-chain interoperability, ZK infrastructure, full-node diversity, post-quantum readiness, or protocol-level threat recovery. Its infrastructure is application-layer DeFi/payment infrastructure, which the Vision 2030 framework places more naturally under Adoption & Utility, especially through DeFi, payments, UX, enterprise security, and developer tooling.

## Classification Notes

### Primary Classification Justification

**Adoption & Utility** was selected because the proposal’s core strategic purpose is to create and deploy a practical on-chain financial primitive that drives real usage: recurring transactions, TVL, enterprise workflows, DeFi composability, payment-like streaming, and integrations by wallets, DAO tooling, custodians, and dApps.

**Infrastructure & Research** was not selected as the Primary Pillar because the proposal does not focus on protocol-level scalability, L1 improvements, L2 integration, cross-chain bridges, ZK capabilities, node/client diversity, or base-layer resilience. The validator, SDK, frontend, and ticker bot are technical deliverables, but their strategic purpose is adoption and utility, not core protocol research or infrastructure hardening.

**Sustainability & Resilience** was also not selected as a Secondary Pillar. The proposal does describe transaction fees flowing back to SPOs and the treasury, but that is an indirect economic effect of usage rather than a direct focus on managed treasury strategy, multi-asset treasury design, L2-to-L1 value retention, or SPO incentive reform under Pillar 5.

### Manual Review Reason

Not applicable.

### Additional Notes

No substantial secondary pillar is assigned. The proposal contains multiple work packages, but they support a single coherent strategic purpose: launching and driving adoption of the Nula token streaming protocol.
