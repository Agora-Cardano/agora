## AI Rationale

The proposal aligns most directly with the Governance pillar because its primary purpose is to create and operate a governance experimentation and evidence-generation mechanism. Its core activities include running two RFP-driven governance cycles, funding governance prototypes and research outputs, coordinating advisors, managing milestone-based grants, publishing public retrospectives, and operating governance-specific tooling such as proposal submission, scoring, milestone tracking, recusal logs, and public decision logs.

The Cardano Vision 2030 Governance pillar focuses on making Cardano governance hard to capture, easy to use, and paced. The proposal directly addresses governance participation, voting-power concentration, delegation stickiness, contributor burnout, evidence-based governance design, advisor accountability, and governance accessibility.

A secondary alignment with Community & Ecosystem is also supported. The proposal includes multilingual and cross-timezone outreach, public workshops, community-facing bounty work, and explicit attention to cultural and translation barriers. These are substantial objectives, not merely incidental benefits. However, they are framed as ways to improve governance participation and legitimacy, rather than as the proposal’s central strategic purpose.

Infrastructure & Research was not selected as the Primary Pillar because the technical platform in WP4 is governance administration and transparency software, not protocol infrastructure, L1 scalability, interoperability, client diversity, or base-layer security work. Sustainability & Resilience was also not selected because, although the proposal includes treasury return conditions and operational controls, it does not primarily focus on treasury asset management, tokenomics, L2-to-L1 value retention, or SPO incentives. Human civilization has once again produced a proposal with four work packages and three self-selected pillars, because apparently one axis of classification was too merciful. Still, the dominant strategic purpose is Governance.

## Supporting Evidence

### Evidence 1

The proposal states that Cardano governance faces problems such as contributor burnout, voting-power concentration, limited participation across cultures and language groups, and the lack of a funded coordination layer for governance interventions.

### Evidence 2

The proposal funds “a coalition of facilitators to run two 6-month RFP-driven governance exercise cycles” producing “PoCs, MVPs, design/research outputs and publicly-reviewable evidence.”

### Evidence 3

WP2 and WP3 are structured as public RFP rounds for governance experimentation, advancing Beyond MVG recommendations from “named” to “prototyped, tested and publicly documented,” with grants paid against milestones and public retrospectives required.

### Evidence 4

WP4 funds a technical platform for RFP submissions, weighted scoring, milestone tracking, retrospective publishing, Look-Back ingestion, advisor dashboards, recusal logs, and a public transparency / decision-log view.

### Evidence 5

The proposal includes cross-community and cross-timezone coordination, native-language outreach, outreach bounties, public workshops, and a recurring requirement to address the cultural / translation challenge, supporting secondary alignment with Community & Ecosystem.

## Classification Notes

### Primary Classification Justification

Governance was selected as the Primary Pillar because the proposal’s main objective, work packages, success metrics, and expected outputs all revolve around governance-specific problems and governance-specific mechanisms. The proposal is not mainly trying to increase DeFi usage, build protocol infrastructure, manage treasury assets, or create a general education program. It is trying to create a structured, transparent, evidence-based pipeline for governance experimentation.

Community & Ecosystem was selected as a Secondary Pillar because the proposal contains substantial outreach and participation-expansion work, especially around language, culture, time zones, workshops, and public engagement. However, those activities support governance inclusion rather than replacing governance as the central purpose.

Infrastructure & Research was not selected because the technical work is application-layer governance tooling, not base-layer protocol infrastructure or research into scalability, interoperability, cryptography, client diversity, or threat recovery.

Sustainability & Resilience was not selected because the proposal’s repayment clauses, operational controls, and time-boxing are governance accountability mechanisms. They do not amount to a primary focus on Cardano’s treasury strategy, tokenomics, L2 value retention, or SPO incentive structure.

### Manual Review Reason

Not required.

### Additional Notes

The proposal has multiple work packages, but they are not strategically fragmented in a way that changes the classification. WP1 coordination, WP2 and WP3 RFP cycles, and WP4 tooling all support the same dominant purpose: governance experimentation, governance evidence generation, and governance process infrastructure.
