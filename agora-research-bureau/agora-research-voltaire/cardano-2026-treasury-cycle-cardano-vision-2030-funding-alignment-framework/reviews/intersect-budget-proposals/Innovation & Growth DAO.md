## AI Rationale

The Innovation & Growth DAO’s core objective is to establish a DRep-governed funding mechanism for Cardano’s treasury, explicitly allocating budget through structured rounds, DRep oversight, and accountability measures. This focus on creating a new on-chain governance process, with DReps reviewing proposals, milestone-based disbursements, and transparent voting, aligns directly with the **Governance** pillar of Cardano Vision 2030, which emphasizes decentralized governance via DReps and structured proposal processes. While the DAO also supports ecosystem sustainability by funding early-stage projects and diversifying funding pathways, these are outcomes of the governance design. The proposal’s description highlights governance features, including DRep review, application rounds, and safeguards, as fundamental, making Governance the best-fit primary pillar.

## Classification Notes

### Primary Classification Justification

The proposal’s central purpose is to create a new governance-driven funding process. It repeatedly emphasizes **DRep oversight, structured voting, and accountability**, which are hallmarks of the Governance pillar. For example, it proposes “DRep-based review” and “transparent reporting” as core deliverables. These features match Pillar 3’s focus on accessible, decentralized governance via DReps. Other pillars, such as Infrastructure or Adoption, do not describe governance mechanisms, and while Sustainability & Resilience relates to funding diversity, the DAO’s description treats sustainability as a consequence rather than the main objective. Thus Governance is the most directly aligned primary pillar.

### Manual Review Reason

Not required. The proposal’s emphasis on DRep governance clearly points to Governance as the primary pillar. Sustainability & Resilience is a secondary effect of this funding mechanism, ensuring smaller projects can grow, but it is not the proposal’s main design goal.
