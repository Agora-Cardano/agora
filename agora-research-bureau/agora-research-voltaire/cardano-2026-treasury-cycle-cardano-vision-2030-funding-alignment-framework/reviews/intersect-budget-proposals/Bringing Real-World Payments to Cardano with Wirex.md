## AI Rationale

This proposal’s core goal is to enable **real-world payments** on Cardano by building a **full-stack payments infrastructure** that connects Cardano to banking rails, stablecoin systems, and Visa/Mastercard networks. It emphasizes merchant acceptance, everyday spending, and fiat on/off-ramps for ADA. These objectives focus on expanding Cardano’s actual utility and usage by end users and businesses. Such goals directly align with the *Adoption & Utility* pillar, which targets high-value verticals like payments and improved user experience. In contrast, the proposal does not focus on base-layer consensus, scalability, or core protocol research. The deliverables are about integrating Cardano into traditional payment channels and enabling ADA spending, not about improving the core protocol itself. Therefore, Adoption & Utility best captures the proposal’s primary strategic purpose.

## Classification Notes

### Primary Classification Justification

The proposal’s primary focus is on enabling Cardano’s use in real-world payments, which is a classic *Adoption & Utility* objective. The Vision 2030 framework explicitly mentions payments as a high-value vertical under the Adoption & Utility pillar. Although the proposal uses on-chain technology, its purpose is to expand utility and user adoption, not to research or enhance the base layer. Therefore, Adoption & Utility is the best match for its strategic purpose.
